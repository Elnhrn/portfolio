A little about me

I was raised in Philadelphia and have lived in Idaho, Utah, Los Angeles, and now live in Sacramento with my 5-year-old son. During the day, I work from home as an affiliate marketing specialist for a comparison shopping engine website. At night, I tackle motherhood and try not to step on rogue Legos (unsuccessfully.)

In my spare time, you can usually find me spending time with my family. I love reading! One year I read 87 books - not including picture books! I also enjoy lifting, riding my bike, EDM, dancing, Netflix, and knitting and crocheting. I try to live a sustainable lifestyle by bringing my own containers and produce bags to the grocery store and limiting the purchase of packaged goods.
